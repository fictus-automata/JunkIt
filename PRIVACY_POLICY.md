# Privacy Policy for JunkIt

**Last Updated: March 21, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how JunkIt ("the App") handles your information. JunkIt is an Android application designed to help users manage "junk" photos and automate their deletion or recycling.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

JunkIt is built with a focus on privacy. **All photo monitoring and processing occur locally on your device.** 

- **Media and Files**: The App monitors your camera directory to identify new photos. If "Junk Mode" is enabled via the Quick Settings Tile, photos captured during that time are tagged for future deletion or removal to the Recycle Bin.
- **No Cloud Storage**: JunkIt does **not** upload your photos, metadata, or any other personal information to any servers or cloud storage services.
- **No Personal Identification**: We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

## 2. Permissions Required

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

- **MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES**: Required to monitor the camera folder and manage (delete/recycle) photos marked as junk.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE**: Used to run a background monitor that detects when new photos are taken while Junk Mode is active.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: Used to notify you when photos are being processed or when cleanup tasks are performed.
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: Allows the App to reschedule its cleanup tasks after your device restarts.

## 3. Data Retention

JunkIt facilitates the deletion of photos you mark as "junk." These photos are either permanently deleted or moved to the Android Recycle Bin (on supported Android versions) based on the App's configuration and your device's capabilities. The App does not retain any copies of these photos outside of their original storage locations.

## 4. Third-Party Services

JunkIt does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

## 5. Security

Since all data processing is local to your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your Android device. We recommend using standard device security features (like screen locks) to protect your information.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
